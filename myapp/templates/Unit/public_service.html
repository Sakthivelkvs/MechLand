Designing

Designing is the basic building block of each product. Design visualises the product aesthetically as well as technically in a virtual world so that we will be able to verify, improvise and rectify the product in the virtual world itself to avoid losses and bottle necks at the time of production. In Mechland,We are having New Products Development (NPD) team with good knowledge in footwear mould of PU, PVC and footwear accessories mould like Footbed mould. All processes are being executed by Computer based CAD/ CAM method with top notch perfection.

Begin Production

The first production order will trigger the installation of the part into our Material Requirements Planning (MRP) system. We establish delivery dates based on material lead times and machine workloads. Frequently, customers send the first production order along with the mold purchase order to eliminate any lead-time issues.
